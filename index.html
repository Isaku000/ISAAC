H2 Kessoku Band

H3 Bocchi the Rock! (ぼっち・ざ・ろっく!, Bocchi za Rokku!) is a Japanese four-panel manga series written and illustrated by Aki Hamaji, serialized in Houbunsha's seinen manga magazine Manga Time Kirara Max since December 2017. It has been collected in five tankōbon volumes as of November 2022. An anime television series adaptation produced by CloverWorks aired from October to December 2022.
